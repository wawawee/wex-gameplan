8.2 Alert Management

Alert Rules


                    groups:
                    - name: trading_alerts
                      rules:
                      - alert: HighOrderLatency
                        expr: order_processing_time > 100
                        for: 5m
                        labels:
                          severity: critical
                        annotations:
                          summary: High order processing latency

                      - alert: SystemOverload
                        expr: cpu_usage > 80 or memory_usage > 85
                        for: 5m
                        labels:
                          severity: warning
                        annotations:
                          summary: System resources near capacity
